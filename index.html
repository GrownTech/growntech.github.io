    WE’RE ON A MISSION TO ACHIEVE URBAN BIODIVERSITY AND CARBON NEUTRALITY


    Grown Tech integrates data from a building's plant management system and other sources, such as weather conditions, irrigation and sunlight and uses artificial intelligence algorithms to optimise plant usage in real time.

    By combining AI with green building standards, we aim to improve occupant well-being, increase operational efficiency, and optimise space and asset utilisation for greening. With our innovative technology, we can accelerate our goal of a truly biophilic city.

    Transforming urban green living with carbon neutral and climate resilient infrastructures
